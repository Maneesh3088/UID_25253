Mysuru Campus

About the Campus

Amrita Mysuru is one of the constituent campuses of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, a NAAC A++ accredited, multidisciplinary, multi-campus university recognized nationally and internationally for academic excellence, research impact, and value-based education.
Located in the heritage city of Mysuru, the campus offers a focused academic environment supported by the academic framework, research ecosystem, and institutional standards of Amrita University.
